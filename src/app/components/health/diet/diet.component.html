Diabetes and Diet

There isn't really a specific diet to follow for people with diabetes. But the foods you eat will make a difference to how successfully your diabetes is managed.

You need to eat a realistic and maintainable diet that fits in with your goals.
These goals could be to:

  • Achieve a healthy weight
  • Reduce cholesterol levels (blood fats)
  • Achieve good blood pressure
  • Cut out/down on Sugars and Fats
  • Reduce Diabetes sugar levels and ultimately remission
